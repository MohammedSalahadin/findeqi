Hi, I'm Rimal Hotel

Rimal Hotel is located in Baghdad, Ideally located near the commercial district of Karada and Arasat Al Hindiya, Free Wi-Fi access is available. It offers a fitness center, a sauna and a restaurant serving international cuisine.

Decorated in red tones, all the rooms and suites are fitted with carpeted floors. Each one offers a balcony, a flat-screen TV and a mini-bar. The suite includes a living room with a dining table. The bathroom is fitted with a bath and a shower.

Guests can visit the National Theater which is 0.6 mi from Rimal Hotel. Baghdad International Airport is 19 mi away. Free private parking is possible on site.
